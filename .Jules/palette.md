## 2025-02-19 - Skip Link & Focus Management
**Learning:** Adding a skip link and programmatic focus management is a low-effort, high-impact a11y win for SPAs.
**Action:** Always check for skip links in new projects and add them if missing. Ensure view transitions manage focus.

## 2025-02-24 - Dynamic ARIA Labels
**Learning:** Dynamically generated icon-only buttons often miss accessible labels, creating invisible barriers in lists and tables.
**Action:** Review all `innerHTML` generation for buttons and ensure `aria-label` or visually hidden text is included.
